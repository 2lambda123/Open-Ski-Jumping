Open Ski Jumping

Welcome at Open Ski Jumping site!

Open Ski Jumping is a free and open source ski jumping 3D video game. Game has an advanced hill generator that allows player to create real-world or fictional venues and share them with other players. You can also create your custom season calendars and share them with other players.

What we are going to add / improve:

  • Add team events
  • Improve hill creator - add more hill construction templates
  • Change jumper physics - add active ragdoll, change model, make better animations
  • Add sound effects
  • Add game translations
  • Add online multiplayer mode
